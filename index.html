About IEEE

Mission

IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

Vision

IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.

About Us

Stony Brook IEEE student branch is an educational and well developed organization for students to pursue their dreams in becoming a well skilled professional engineer. Our goal is to spread the knowledge of theory and support undergraduate and graduate students who are interested in Electrical and Computer Science Engineering with field related resources.
